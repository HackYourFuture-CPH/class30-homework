My Favorite Food: Gheymeh

Gheymeh is a traditional Persian stew made with split peas, tender chunks of meat, and flavored with spices, served with rice. It’s a delicious and hearty dish that is often enjoyed in Iranian cuisine.

Ingredients

- 1 cup split peas
- 300 grams lamb or beef, cut into small cubes
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2-3 cups water or broth
- 2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- Fried potatoes (for garnish)
- Saffron (optional, for aroma and color)
- Persian rice (for serving)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Split Peas: 
   - Rinse the split peas under cold water and set them aside.

2. Cook the Meat: 
   - In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
   - Add the meat cubes and cook until browned on all sides.

3. Add Spices and Tomatoes: 
   - Stir in the turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add the chopped tomatoes and tomato paste, cooking for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft.

4. Add Split Peas and Water: 
   - Add the rinsed split peas to the pot, followed by water or broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 1.5 hours, or until the meat and peas are tender. Stir occasionally and add more water if needed.

5. Serve: 
   - Once the stew is ready, adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot over Persian rice, garnished with fried potatoes and a sprinkle of saffron, if desired.

Enjoy your delicious Gheymeh!

About Gheymeh
Gheymeh is not just a dish; it’s a celebration of flavors and traditions in Persian culture. It's often served during special occasions and gatherings, making it a beloved favorite among many.
